Nil Biescas, Josep Lladós, Sanket Biswas
CVPR, 2025

A method for structured document understanding without relying on language or OCR. By combining geometric edge features and visual features in a two-stage graph attention framework, this model achieves strong performance on link prediction and entity recognition.
